# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want this project to be a friendly, practical place for people building on
ESP32-P4 hardware. We welcome contributors regardless of experience level,
background, location, or identity.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, especially when debugging hardware issues.
- Share enough context for others to reproduce your results.
- Keep discussions focused on the project and the technical problem at hand.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeated off-topic comments after a maintainer asks to refocus.
- Any behavior that makes the project hostile for contributors or users.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove comments, close issues, reject contributions,
or block participants who violate this code of conduct.

To report a problem, contact the maintainers through the repository support
channels or the project owner's published contact channel.
